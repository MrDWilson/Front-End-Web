What We Do

Here at Philip Lewis Contruction, we strive for greatness. Whether it be a simple renovation, or an extension doubling your house size.

  • Extensions - No matter how large the task, we can execute solutions of the highest quality.

  • Renovations - Ever had that one room in your house that you dreamed could be something else? We can make that happen. Games room? Combining Kitchen and Dining room? Reconfigure the houses layout completely? You've came to the right place. We make dreams happen. Turn your house into the home you deserve.

  • Conservatories - Conservatories are a great and easy way to make an extension to your house. Huge amounts of natural light make for a great reading room, or even an extension to an existing Kitchen.

  • Roofing and Tiling - Has a storm hit recently, and the damage is both an eyesore and an inconvience? We can help! We can fufill your roofing needs, whatever they are, and get your roof looking new again!

  • Garden - Always dreamed of a getaway style garden, but the time taken is just too much? Let us do it for you! You will spend every moment in your new garden wondering why you hadn't had it done sooner!

  • Electrics and plumbing - We employ multiple workers in both fields, so no matter how small the task, get in touch!


We do the best we can to serve all customers to the greatest of our abilities. If the work you are looking to have completed is not listed above, please contact us directly here for a personal consultation.
